NICE1: Cold + Compression Therapy System

The VPulse cold compression device is a medical device designed to provide pain relief and reduce swelling in an injured area of the body. It is commonly used by athletes, post-operative patients, and people with chronic conditions such as arthritis.

The VPulse device consists of a control unit and a variety of wraps or sleeves designed to fit specific body parts such as the knee, ankle, elbow, or shoulder. The control unit houses a reservoir for ice and water, a pump, and a timer.

Using the VPulse device involves filling the reservoir with ice and water, then connecting the appropriate wrap or sleeve to the control unit. Once the wrap or sleeve is secured in place over the affected area, the user can activate the device to begin the cold compression therapy. The device applies cold water to the affected area while simultaneously inflating and deflating the wrap or sleeve, providing compression to the area.

Many patients don’t realize that their body needs more nutrients than usual when they undergo surgery. That is why 47% of Orthopedic surgery patients are nutritionally deficient.

Just by supplementing your body with the right nutrition, and at the right time, you can experience a better surgical outcome. Therefore, your surgeon recommends the Ortho Nutrition Bundle before and after your surgery to help you prepare better and recover faster.

What is the Ortho Nutrition Bundle?

The Ortho Nutrition Bundle is a 2-weeks pre and 2-weeks post surgery nutritional supplementation program that has been developed with leading nutrition researchers and orthopedic specialists to help you meet the elevated nutritional needs around surgery.

The bundle contains 56 servings of ISOlution® and 2 servings of PREcovery®, along with a shaker bottle and a detailed guidebook. The bundle is recommended to be started 2 weeks prior to your surgery and continued for 2 weeks after surgery for best results.

How It Helps

The bundle is designed to be used around the surgical period and can help in 3 ways:

  1. Prepare before surgery: Just like an athlete needs to prepare for a marathon, your body needs to prepare for a major event like a surgery. ISOlution® feeds the body with high-quality protein that helps in preparing your body to go into surgery.
  2. Prime for surgery: PREcovery® helps in optimizing your body’s metabolism to experience lesser unwanted side effects and better post-surgical outcomes.
  3. Recover after surgery: After surgery, your body needs to recuperate effectively before it can return to normal. Isolation® helps fuel the body with the nutrients required to bounce back faster

The Benefits

The Ortho Nutrition Bundle can help you maintain muscle, improve wound healing and facilitate a rapid recovery from surgery.

With this bundle, you have access to clinical nutrition designed specifically for orthopedic surgery. This 4-week program can help you bounce back stronger and faster. Your journey to a healthy recovery starts here
